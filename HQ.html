Harley Quinn: The Animated Series

Harley Quinn: The Animated Series is an American adult animated black comedy superhero television series based on the DC Comics character of the same name created by Paul Dini and Bruce Timm. The series follows the misadventures of Harley Quinn and her best friend (and ultimately turned girlfriend), Poison Ivy, after leaving her boyfriend, the Joker.

The show premiered on DC Universe to critical acclaim in 2019, with critics praising its animation, humor, dark tone, voice acting, and portrayal of the titular protagonist. Kaley Cuoco and Lake Bell voice Harley Quinn and Poison Ivy, respectively.
